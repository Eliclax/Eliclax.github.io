Welcome to the Mountain of Mathematics!

At school, teachers train students in the Gym of Mathematics. It's healthy, but it's repetitive and not necessarily practical. As a guide on this proverbial Mountain of Mathematics, I hope to show students that climbing this mountain helps you see further while also developing the mental muscles that are truly important in this day and age: creative problem-solving skills. Of course, there are many practical benefits to climbing this mountain, but at the end of the day, the best reason to climb a mountain is that the journey is fun and the views are beautiful, and that is what I ultimately hope students take away.

Featuring:

  • Fully-developed curricula, taught through year-long courses.
  • Regular content review and practice contests to track progress.
  • Weekly office hours for all students.
  • Contest live-solving sessions.
